Pricing Analysis

Is this a novel product? Yes. The market has driftwood lamps (fairy lights, Edison bulbs — no programmability) and LED art sculptures (manufactured geometric forms — acrylic, metal, resin). The intersection of natural fallen wood + addressable programmable LEDs + audio-reactive/interactive is genuinely rare. Almost nothing combines all three.

Strategy: Start with 2–3 mass-producible medium pieces at $200–250 to build reviews. The audio-reactive / bespoke tier is the real differentiator — once you have traction, the $500–1,000+ pieces are where the margin lives. Nobody else is combining natural wood forms with programmable addressable LEDs, which gives genuine pricing power.
